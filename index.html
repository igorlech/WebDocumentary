WELCOME TO THE COMMUNITY

The Multimedia Design building is known as ''Golden Tooth'', but we want you discover Business Academy Aarhus' golden heart (please excuse the cheesy pun, we couldn't help ourselves).

BAA's mission is to educate whole people, whilst adhering to their values of being fast, focused, flexible, friendly and fun. But further to this, at it's core, BAA is a community which values inclusion.

Made up of students from all over the world, at BAA, you are guaranteed to find your place in our community.
