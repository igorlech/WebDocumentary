WELCOME TO THE COMMUNITY

The Multimedia Design building is known as ''Golden Tooth''', but we want you discover Business Academy Aarhus' golden heart (please excuse the cheesy pun, we couldn't help ourselves!)

We know that starting out at a new school can be daunting, especially if you've just moved from another country. You wonder what the place is going to be like, how you'll go about making new friends, or maybe wondering if you'll fit in.

These feelings are normal. However, Business Academy Aarhus will welcome you with open arms. Our mission is to educate whole people, whilst adhering to our values of being fast, focused, flexible, friendly and fun. But further to this, at its core, Business Academy Aarhus is a community which values inclusion.

Made up of students from all over the world, at Business Academy Aarhus, you are guaranteed to find your place in our community.

Click and find out what our community thinks about inclusion:

Inclusion means different things to different people. For some it means accepting others. For others it means communities of diverse people thriving together.

At Business Academy Aarhus we want you to be YOU, no matter who that is! By becoming a part of our community, you can form your own understanding of inclusive education, whilst connecting with people from all over the world!
